Problem Statement & Context

The Challenge

  • AI systems now outperform humans in CAPTCHA tests (99.8% vs 71-85%)
  • Deepfakes can defeat biometric authentication
  • AGI threatens democratic processes, social media, financial systems
"How do we prove someone is human in the AGI era?"

The Proof of Personhood Trilemma

Decentralization

No single point of control

Scalability

Works for millions of users

AGI-Resistance

Can't be fooled by AI

Current Reality

Systems can only achieve 2 out of 3

  • Worldcoin: AGI-resistant + Scalable, but Centralized
  • BrightID: Decentralized + Scalable, but vulnerable to AGI

Our Three Proposed Solutions

PMAP (Privacy-Maximizing Attestation Protocol)

  • Hardware security + Zero-knowledge proofs
  • Best for: High-security financial systems

HAP (Hybrid Assurance Protocol)

  • Multi-factor: Biometric + Social + Behavioral
  • Best for: General-purpose platforms

DAP (Decentralized Auditor Protocol)

  • Peer-to-peer verification + Economic incentives
  • Best for: Democratic voting, censorship-resistant systems
